GENERAL

If I use your music, will I get flagged for copyright on YouTube or elsewhere?

Nope! The music isn't registered for mechanical collection anywhere. If you do get flagged, someone other than me has registered the music as their own. Bad person. Please let me know if you find a bad person.

Can I use your music for free in my gaming video, animation, promo video, podcast...etc?

Short answer: probably. View the above chart to double-check. The music is all CC-BY. If it says 'ONLY WITH CREDIT', then you have to add this link to the description of your video or podcast: Music by John Bartmann https://johnbartmann.com/

Can I use your music in a branded video on YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Facebook etc?

Yes. Check the chart above for usage scenarios.

Do I need to ask permission to use your music in my commercial projects?

No. But you have credit me. If you can't credit me, purchase a license. View the chart above. Here's a ratecard for all usage scenarios.

What is the license type of your music?

Creative Commons CC-BY license. Get in touch if you're unsure about what that means.

Do you own the rights to reproduce this music? 

Yes. I've done some Christmas stuff, but other than that, I am the 100% sound recording and compositional rights holder. everything else was either written and recorded by me or someone I've hired.

Can I monetize my YouTube videos?

Yes.

Can I resell your music?

No. It's terrible karma. All tracks have their own ISRC numbers (digital fingerprints), so I am able to prove that I'm the original author.

CREDITING & DOWNLOADS

If I want to credit you, how exactly do I do it?

Add this to your project or episode description: Music by John Bartmann https://johnbartmann.com/

Do I need to credit you if I purchase the music?

If you pay, no.

How do I download MIDI, sheet music or MusicXML?

Contact me directly for any of the above.

Am I able to re-use the music multiple times?

Yes. For example, you may use a track as theme music for every episode of your podcast, like these awesome people do.

Can I please have a written license documentation with my purchase?

Most people don't need a written license anymore. But if you do, contact me.

Am I able to specify a license holder other than myself?

Yes.

Can you compose new music specifically for my project?

I'd love to! Contact me with a proposal to write some original music.

How much do you charge to write original music?

I generally charge according to how urgently you need the composition. Contact me and let's chat. I promise I won't bankrupt you.

Can I submit my own music to be published on your site?

Nope, sorry. But there are loads of other sites where you can.

PAYMENT AND TECHNICAL

How do I pay you or buy something from you?

1. By purchasing the track/product/asset pack via Gumroad (using PayPal or credit card). You will then have direct access to the files. 2. By paying me directly via PayPal. You will then need to request that I send you the files.

Am I able to download music on an iPad or iPhone?

No, you'll need to use a desktop computer, laptop or Android device.
